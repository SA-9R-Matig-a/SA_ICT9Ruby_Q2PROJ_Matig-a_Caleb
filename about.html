About

Capanna Italiana is a website that shows off Italy's finest quisines. We carry and proudly spread Italy's best recipes for all to enjoy, with a dedicated recipe form if you'd like to share some of your own Italian recipes!


In our fine little cozy cabin, we hope you can find comfort in these dishes, as well as ecstasy as you chow down on our delicious food. Your satisfaction brings satisfaction to every Italian grandmother who passed down generations of recipes, which has brought been brought to you.


We hold:

  • Delicious Recipes
  • Simple and Easy Instructions
  • A Mode to Express Your Favorite Recipes!

If you're not a cook, then no problem! We have easy-to-learn recipes! Just follow what's said and you'll be able to cook a dish even Gordon Ramsay would cry from joy!
